Who Are We ?

Awfer is a next-generation mobility and service infrastructure designed to give cities, drivers, and users full control over how transportation and on-demand services operate.

We are not a ride-hailing app. We are a technology platform that provides the digital rails for independent transport networks to exist without dependency on global intermediaries.

Our system allows operators, cooperatives, and local fleets to run their own fully functional service, including booking, routing, pricing, driver management, and customer interaction, while remaining connected to a unified, secure, and scalable ecosystem.

Unlike centralized platforms that own the data, the users, and the revenue flow, Awfer is built on decentralization, ownership, and transparency. Every participant, whether a driver, fleet owner, or service operator, retains control over their identity, earnings, and operational data.

Awfer combines real time networking, secure communications, geolocation services, and automated dispatch systems into a lightweight and efficient infrastructure that can operate even in regions where traditional platforms fail or are unavailable.

Our mission is simple. Give mobility back to the people who provide it and the cities that rely on it.

Awfer is the foundation for a fair, open, and truly global service economy.

By controlling how we build, scale, and operate, we unlock fairness in a market where it has been structurally impossible.
